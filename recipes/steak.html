Sous Vide Steak

The perfect way to cook a Juicy steak.

A perfect tender steak that you won't need a knife to cut the meat.

Ingredients:

  • New Your Strip Steak
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Anova Sous Vide or other Sous Vide machine

Steps:

  1. Fill a pot with water
  2. put the sous vide machine into the pot and preheat the device to 130 degrees
  3. put the steak into a ziploc bag.
  4. once the water reaches the temperature put the steak into the pot
  5. let it sit in the water for 1 hour
  6. after the hour, take the steak out and dry with paper towel
  7. salt and peper the steak on both side
  8. preheat the pan with olive oil on high
  9. sear the steak on both side for 1-2 minutes
  10. serve the steak with desired sides
