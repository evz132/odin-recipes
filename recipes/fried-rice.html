10 minute Easy Egg Fried Rice!

The easist fried rice you'll ever make

Follow the recipe below for a quick and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • a wok if not a deeper walled frying pan
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups of left over rice
  • 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots
  • soy sauce
  • dark soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat and oil the wok or pan and set the temperture to medium to medium high
  2. mince the garlic
  3. fry the garlic very quick to not burn it
  4. put the rice in
  5. put the frozen vegetables in
  6. move the rice to the side, and put the eggs in and scramble the eggs
  7. put both of the soy sauces in
  8. stir the rice till the sauce in incorprated with the rice
  9. serve fresh
